Our Heart

Rooted in the biblical call to love our neighbors as ourselves, MAFS is a collective of believers dedicated to the ministry of restoration. We believe that honoring God requires excellence in our craft; we use professional precision to deliver Christ-led compassion to the marginalized.

The Mission

To provide essential resources and sustainable support to marginalized families globally.

The Vision

A future where every individual has the opportunity to thrive and smile with dignity.

Transparency

Every shilling is accounted for and tracked. Stewardship is our highest form of worship.

Compassion

We see brothers and sisters made in the image of God, leading with empathy in every hand extended.

Sustainability

We build systems—like skills training and savings circles—that empower the future.
